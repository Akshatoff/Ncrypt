Craze.Create.Connect

CRUX is the flagship tech event by the Delhi Tech Circuit (DTC), a coalition of computer clubs across Delhi. The name represents the core of technologyâ€”where innovation and creativity meet to solve real-world challenges.

Our event brings together students to explore cutting-edge tech through hands-on workshops and competitions. From AI and robotics to drones and 3D modeling, CRUX offers interactive experiences that ignite passion for tech.

The Delhi Tech Circuit (DTC) is all about building a thriving tech community. CRUX will create opportunities for collaboration between students, schools, and industry experts, helping foster the next generation of tech leaders. By bringing real-world tech challenges to students, CRUX empowers them to develop the skills and connections needed to contribute to and shape the future of the tech ecosystem.

Associate Members

Pyrotech

Pyrotech, a group of student tech enthusiasts, excels in competitions from web design to robotics, showcasing diverse skills. Their achievements in image editing and filmmaking highlight their dedication. Ordin@Trix, evolving as Pyrotech's core, pushes the boundaries of tech exploration and innovation through their efforts.

nCrypt

nCrypt, a dynamic group of student coders and innovators, specializes in competitions ranging from cryptic hunts to gaming (and casting - shoutout Mehul Raina). Their success in hackathons and coding challenges reflects their commitment to problem-solving and technological advancement. As Ncrypt continues to grow, it remains at the forefront of fostering creativity and skill in the tech community.

Tech Syndicate

Established in 2016, Tech Syndicate is the technology club of Amity International School, Sector-46, Gurgaon. The club works constantly to encourage students to learn new technologies and keep up with the rapidly progressing sector of computer science.
